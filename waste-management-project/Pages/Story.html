Our Story

We are a team of IT enthusiasts looking forward to bringing knowledge, solutions and awareness to the public on waste management plus skills and benefits. This is basically going to be achieved various forms of electronic waste management techniques. This initiative has been undertaken after thorough research on waste and its adverse effects on our environment. We have started it with a goal of achieving greater awareness of waste management to a greater population of the public, to conserve our environment and to improve on waste disposal habits of people.

Objectives

  • To create awareness to the vast population through the available technology
  • To educate people about the dangers of improper waste disposal
  • To make use of the available provisions to support proper waste management
  • To educate people about the benefits of recycling.
